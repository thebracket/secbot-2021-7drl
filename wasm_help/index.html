SecBot (2021 7DRL) - by Herbert Wolverson


SecBot is my 7-day Roguelike entry for 2021. Written in 7 days, using only techniques from my book Hands-On Rust.

SecBot is the remains of a dead human, stuffed full of cybernetics, and forced into service with Bracket Corp. SecBot is a pleasant fellow, and doesn't really want to kill everyone - but it's up to you if you want to honor that desire.

Move around with either the cursor keys or WASD. There is no diagonal movement.

You can mouse-over anything to see its description.

You can cycle between available targets by pressing T.

Fire your weapon by pressing F.
